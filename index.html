Computer Vision Engine

Precision through real-time skeletal tracking.

E-Track leverages advanced computer vision to track skeletal markers in real-time. We don't just record video; we transform each frame into quantifiable movement data. Our engine focuses on force distribution, joint kinematics, and structural integrity during high-impact phases like CMJ, Drop Jumps, and Pogo Jumps. By eliminating manual tracking, we provide the objective metrics to diagnose movement inefficiencies instantly.
🚀

Zero Logistics

True portability for any training environment.

Forget bulky force plates, sensor-heavy suits, or complex camera rigs. E-Track democratizes high-level biomechanical analysis by turning any smartphone into a professional-grade lab. Whether you are operating in a professional high-performance center, a local gym, or directly on the training field, you only need your device and our software to initiate a full-scale analysis. We have removed the hardware friction so you can focus entirely on the athlete.
🧪

Scientific Validation

Data that speaks the language of science.

Move beyond subjective guesswork. Every session generates a comprehensive, reproducible data set—from RSI and Contact Time to relative force production. These metrics are processed via API to ensure millimetric precision, creating a permanent, scientific record of your athlete’s progression, allowing you to prove the effectiveness of your training protocols with absolute certainty.
