Data is processed using electronic tools designed to ensure security and confidentiality, respecting adequate technical and organizational measures (such as authentication protocols and secure connections). We do not engage in automated decision-making or intrusive user profiling.

Data will be stored only for the duration strictly necessary to achieve the aforementioned purposes and in any case no longer than 24 months from collection, unless a professional or contractual relationship is established.

6. User Rights

Pursuant to Articles 15-22 of the GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, delete, or limit the processing of your data. To exercise these rights, please send a written request to: info@e-track-lab.com.
